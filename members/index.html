Our Team

ICRF is a network of cancer researchers, doctors, and volunteers working to combat the proliferation of cancer in Kenya. Our membership comprises volunteers working both on the ground in Kenya and contributing to our efforts from abroad. This includes doctors in oncology researchers, medical doctors, nurses, and PhD candidates in cancer-related research programs.
