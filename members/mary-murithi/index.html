Mary Murithi, MSc

Ethics Manager

Linkedin

Mary is a PhD Scholar pursuing Biochemistry at Jomo Kenyatta University of Agriculture &
Technology, Kenya. She holds an MSc in Biochemistry and a BSc in Biochemistry & Molecular
Biology from the same institution. Her research interests focus on exploring the genetic
polymorphisms in metabolic enzymes and assessing the role of these mutations in the development
of breast cancer in women of African descent. The aim is to develop strategies that are
specific and effective to the target population for prevention, screening, early diagnosis, and
provision of evidence-based policy advisories. Her other areas of interest include
bioinformatics, research methods, and bioethics. Currently, Mary works as an assistant
lecturer in the School of Pharmacy at Kabarak University, Kenya.
