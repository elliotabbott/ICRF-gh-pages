About Us

Welcome To ICRF-Kenya

Our mission is to address the burden of cancer in Kenya through evidence-based research. We believe in prevention as the first line of defense against this disease. By promoting awareness, we hope to encourage early diagnosis and testing that will enhance timely management of the disease.

Saving lives through cutting-edge research and evidence based awareness

What We Do

Our Aims

Research

Our research aims to inform cancer eradication policy by characterizing the impact and state of cancer in Kenya. We focus on research that provides social and epidemiological insights for the immediate future.
Learn More

Outreach

Our work intends to systematically and consistently engage and support the public in addressing their needs to access efficient cancer management services.
Learn More

Clinical

ICRF aims to play a role in supporting access to standardized cancer care through improving professional networking, and supporting urban and rural access to resources and technology.
