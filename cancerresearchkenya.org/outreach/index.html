Public Outreach

Background

Cancer Research Kenya will fully embody public engagement and work with local communities to create long-lasting and fruitful solutions to the problem of cancer in Kenya. We will systematically and consistently engage and support the public in addressing their needs to access efficient cancer management services, including information on cancer healthcare facilities, sharing our research work and nurturing cancer research culture.

Focus Areas

We will engage with local residents, both individuals and community groups, and the wider public; patients; academic institutions and research groups; other stakeholders and the Kenyan government through seminars and conferences.

Cancer Awareness

The Cancer Awareness Department is responsible for cancer awareness and education sessions and activities that are organized in a bid to educate the public on various cancer types as well as promote prevention and early screening among members of the public.

Cancer awareness sessions are conducted monthly and in-person in various locations in various counties and institutions in line with the needs associated with a locality. Online awareness sessions are also available and will be hosted on our social channels in a bid to expand outreach to many more members of the public. 

Community Engagement

The Community Engagement department is responsible for creating awareness about resource offerings available at public health facilities in a bid to assist and support more members of the public in accessing screening and treatment services currently available at the facilities.

Fostering public participation and engagement within Kenya’s cancer health infrastructure will improve on public engagement which is key in carrying out medical research, awareness and education and building discourse on how to improve and support both patients and facilities in offering cancer care services.  

Access & Education

Oncology research remains an underrepresented field in Kenya. The department of Access and Education aims to support young and upcoming researchers to engage in cancer research through creating awareness on research opportunities in the field of cancer.

The department will organize training and learning events, coordinate placements and internships in a bid to enhance skills, knowledge, and capacity in efforts towards bridging the gaps in cancer research.
